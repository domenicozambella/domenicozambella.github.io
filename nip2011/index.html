Mini-school in Model Theory

Torino, 9-11 February 2011

Aula S, Palazzo Campana, via Carlo Alberto 10

Enrique Casanovas and Hans Adler will lecture about the basic theory of NIP. A few short talks will also be organized.

Participation is open to all interested in attending. Contact: domenico.zambella@unito.it.
